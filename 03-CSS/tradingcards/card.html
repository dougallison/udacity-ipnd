Humpback Whale

Male humpbacks vocalize long songs during the breeding season; each song can last 10 to 20 minutes and vary in amplitude and frequency.

  • Scientific Name: Megaptera novaeangliae
  • Average Length: 13 - 16 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 45 - 50 years
  • Habitat: All major oceans

Humpback whales are a species of baleen whale that inhabit all of the world's major oceans. Adult whales can weigh up to 66,000 pounds and reach a length up to 52 feet. Once on the brink of extinction, there are now an estimated 80,000 humpback whales in population.
